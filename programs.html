Explorers Club

Mentorship program designed for children ages 7-11, encouraging them to explore new ideas, develop essential life skills, and discover their passions. Through fun activities, engaging projects, and group mentoring, the Explorers Club provides a supportive environment where kids can grow, learn, and build confidence. With the guidance of caring mentors, participants embark on a journey of self-discovery, teamwork, and leadership, setting them up for a future full of possibilities.

Pathfinder

A mentorship initiative designed to guide and inspire children ages 12-14 on their journey of personal growth, learning, and exploration. By connecting young minds with dedicated mentors, the program helps kids discover their strengths, build confidence, and develop essential skills for the future.

Pathway to success

Mentoring program designed to guide high school and post high school aged students through the important decisions of their academic and career journeys. Whether exploring GED, college or trade school options, we aim to provide personalized support to help students discover their strengths, plan their futures, and make informed choices about post-high school opportunities. Through workshops, one-on-one coaching, and expert advice.

Parenting Together

A coaching program that emphasizes the power of community and support in raising happy, healthy children. Understanding that parenting is a shared journey, this program offers parents the tools, strategies, and encouragement they need to thrive. Through one-on-one coaching, group discussions, and a supportive network, Parenting Together fosters connection and collaboration among parents, creating a "village" of shared wisdom and guidance. Parents will learn effective communication, advocate, and how to build strong, positive relationships with their children.
